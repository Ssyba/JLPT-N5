ここ
 here, this place
You are looking for something, and someone points to a cocoa (hot chocolate) cup and says, "This cocoa (koko) here is yours."
Remember how words beginning with こ are close to you? It ends with こ too, which is a clue that we’re talking about a place. So ここ means here.
Wanikani
2
そこ
there, that place
You are looking for something, and someone points to a sock over there and says, "There, that sock (soko) is yours."
Remember how words beginning with そ are either close to the person you're talking to, or fairly close to both of you? And how words ending in こ refer to places? This one's no exception. そこ means there.
