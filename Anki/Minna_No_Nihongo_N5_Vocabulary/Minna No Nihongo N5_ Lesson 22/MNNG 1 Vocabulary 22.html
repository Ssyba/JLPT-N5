(シャツを)きます
wear, put on (a shirt, dress etc)
What do you want to wear today? How about a dress made entirely of keys (き)? Feel the metal on your skin and the weight on your shoulders. It's not a comfortable thing to put on. Maybe just stick with wearing a single key around your neck next time.

The hat you want to put on is way too heavy for you. It falls off your head and hits the ground with a loud KABOOM (かぶ). You try to wear the hat again and pull it down tight, but this time it pulls you to the ground with an even louder KABOOM!
4
(めがねを)かけます
wear, put on, hang (glasses)
You can remember this because you must care (ける) about the way you hang something up. Without care, it won't stay hanging for long.
5
うまれます

Something is about to be born, and when you lean in for a closer look… it’s an uni (う). Yes, a sea urchin. You weren’t expecting that. Close your eyes and imagine what the process of an uni being born might look like, to help you remember this reading.
